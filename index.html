About

GetPeppers is a small business located in Richland, Washington that grows and sells the world’s hottest pepper, the Carolina Reaper. Here at GetPeppers we are committed to providing our customers with the hottest pepper money can buy. The owner of GetPeppers, Dan Avila, personally guarantees all of his peppers to be unreasonably spiced.

Our Peppers

Here at GetPeppers we are committed to providing the Pacific Northwest with the world's hottest pepper, the infamous Carolina Reaper. With over 2 million Scoville Heat Units (SHU), the Carolina Reaper is feared and praised by many. It's over four hundred times hotter than the jalepeno pepper and twice as hot as the notorius Bhut Jolokia (Ghost Pepper). Though many have tried, few can say that they have conquered the Reaper.

Our Carolina Reapers are grown from seed here in Richland, Washington; enabling us to distribute them at extremely low prices. These peppers are carefully cultivated and harvested over a nine month growing period to ensure thorough spiciness.
